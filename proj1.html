> (1) Experiments in programming & interface design

DESCRIPTION: These are my solutions to the exercises provided in Brian P. Hogan's "Exercises for Programmers". Instead of providing straight forward (and boring) solutions I've tried to play with color, interaction and even storytelling to make their presentation more engaging.

My aim is to translate these exercises into other interactive mediums (e.g. AR, VR, mobile) as my skills progress. Ultimately though, I want to use these skills to tell and create engaging and immersive stories and interactions.
