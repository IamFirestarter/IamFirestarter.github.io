Why number42 and that triangle logo?

I like to think that little triangle as my own hypersigil. To me it symbolizes growth and my journey into achieving and fulfilling what I call "Torvald's law". As for the number, you probably should read The Hitchhiker's Guide to the Galaxy to find out why. In a nutshell though, 42 is the answer to the meaning of life in the sense that it's not the meaning of life that is important, but the meaning we assign it. A lot of people fall into downward spirals they can't ever seem to get out of because they focus too much on trying to find the right answers, when what they should be doing is asking the right questions. Questions like, what fun activities and experiences do I enjoy doing? And how can I spend more time around people that inspire me, and make me laugh and smile? 42 is about asking the right questions which lead to the right answers: answers you and me give meaning and value to.
